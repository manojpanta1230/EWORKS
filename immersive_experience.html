Immersive Experience

TBC SCWU has collaborated with UpaCare Health to address the spectrum of your mental health and well-being needs. Harnessing the latest technology and established by a team of medical professionals and computer scientists, UpaCare Health is dedicated to delivering accessible and comprehensive healthcare solutions. As a part of your mental health and wellbeing unit, UpaCare Health aims to create a supportive and inclusive environment where students can thrive academically and personally while addressing their mental health needs.

Cluster B: Communities

Welcome to ‘Into Work Chicago Source’ Counselling and Mentoring Unit (ITSC SOURC) – a support service for better health and wellbeing. Our expert team of counselors, social workers, and peer mentors understand the challenges faced by individuals within communities, delivering tailored mental health support and wellbeing strategies.

ITSC SOURC focuses on building trust through active listening, problem solving, and personalized care plans. The center also runs several health awareness workshops, employment mentoring, and youth programs to empower communities.

Ultimately, ITSC SOURC’s mission is dedicated to enabling individuals, especially young adults, to successfully transition into employment by addressing their unique challenges, while also helping them build resilience, confidence, and sustainable support networks.

Sports & Wellness

Welcome to ‘The Inner College Student Counselling and Mentoring Unit (ITSC SOURCE)’ – your trusted space for Mental Health and Wellbeing. The experienced team of licensed health professionals ensures trusted consultations, encouraging open and honest communication, while helping students to improve mental health and overall well-being.

ITSC SOURCE’s wellness program, ‘LifeCenter Health,’ is dedicated to improving student lives by addressing the spectrum of their mental health and physical health needs, maintaining the delicate balance between study, work, and life. Our team of medical professionals and counselors at LifeCenter Health is dedicated to delivering personalized care.
