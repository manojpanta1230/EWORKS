Special Culinary Event: Expert
Thai Chef from Hyatt Regency

Thai culinary maestro

September 10,2023
10:00 am
The British Brasserie
Anish Nepal

SEO Manager

We are thrilled to announce an upcoming event at The British College that is set to be a delectable learning experience! On Sunday, September 10th, we have the honor of hosting the esteemed Chef Siriporn Krasae-at, an expert Thai culinary maestro from the renowned Hyatt Regency. Our Level-5 BHM students will have the unique opportunity to glean insights and techniques from her exceptional expertise. The event will take place at The British Brasserie, starting at 10 AM and concluding at 2 PM. Don't miss out on this educational and delicious affair!
